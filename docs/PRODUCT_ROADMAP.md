# LinkPocket Product Roadmap

## Product Vision

LinkPocket evolves from a **link saver** into a **personal AI knowledge
archive**.

Goal:

Saved information → Structured knowledge.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 1 --- Foundation

-   UI polish
-   design system stabilization
-   component refactor
-   router implementation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 2 --- Knowledge Structure

Introduce structured knowledge layers:

Topics Knowledge cards Insights

Example:

Topic: AI

Articles Insights Timeline

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 3 --- AI Research Tools

AI-generated insights:

Key insights Why it matters Contrarian view Related topics

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 4 --- Search & Discovery

-   semantic search
-   topic search
-   command palette (Cmd + K)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 5 --- Reader Mode

Features:

-   clean article reader
-   highlights
-   notes
-   reading progress

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 6 --- Knowledge Graph

Graph view of topics and articles.

Example:

AI ├ LLM ├ OpenAI └ AI regulation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Phase 7 --- Knowledge Synthesis

Users can ask questions about stored knowledge.

Example:

"What is happening in offshore wind in Korea?"

AI generates:

-   summary
-   timeline
-   key insights
